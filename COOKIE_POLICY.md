Cookie Policy

Effective date: January 25, 2026

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how The Fashion Gallery uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. Cookies help us deliver a better experience by remembering preferences and gathering analytics.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites. They can be "session" cookies (expire when you close your browser) or "persistent" (remain until they expire or you delete them).

3. Types of Cookies We Use

- Strictly necessary: Required for site functionality (cart, secure areas). These are always active.
- Performance & analytics: Help us understand how visitors use the site (e.g., page views, navigation). We use this data to improve the site.
- Functional: Remember preferences such as language and display settings.
- Marketing & advertising: Used to deliver relevant ads and measure campaign effectiveness. These may be set by third parties.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use trusted third-party services (analytics providers, payment partners, social widgets) that may set cookies on your device. Each third party has its own cookie policy and controls.

5. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality (e.g., cart, login). To manage cookies specifically:
- In Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
- In Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- In other browsers: consult the browser’s help resources.

6. Consent

By using our site you consent to the use of cookies as described. Where required by law, we will present a cookie consent banner to obtain your consent for non-essential cookies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy. We will post changes here with an updated effective date.
